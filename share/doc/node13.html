Introduction

PICSimLab means PIC Simulator Laboratory

PICSimLab is a realtime emulator of development boards with integrated MPLABX/avr-gdb debugger. PICSimLab supports picsim microcontrollers: PIC16F84/16F628/16F648/16F777/16F877A/18F452/18F4520/18F4550/18F4620 and simavr microcontroller: ATMEGA328. PICSimLab have integration with MPLABX/Arduino IDE for programming the boards microcontrollers.
